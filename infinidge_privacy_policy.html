Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025
Infinidge is built on a simple principle: you come here to learn something surprising every day, not to be tracked. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, why, and what we don't do with it.

1. Who we are

Infinidge is an independent app developed and published by Infinidge (developer account on Google Play). If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at the address at the bottom of this page.

2. What data we collect

Infinidge collects minimal data. Here is everything we store:

Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Your daily streak count and best streak
  • Total number of facts you have seen
  • The date you last opened the app (for streak calculation)
  • Facts you have bookmarked (saved locally)
  • A list of fact topics already shown (to avoid repeats)
  • Your notification time preference
  • Your display mode preference (dark or light)
  • Whether you have completed onboarding
  • Your Premium status (purchased or earned via streak)
  • Number of streak shields held
  • Milestones already unlocked

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to our servers, sold to third parties, or used for advertising profiling. It exists solely to make the app work correctly for you.

3. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files
  • We do not build a profile about you
  • We do not sell your data — ever

4. AI-generated content

Infinidge uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate facts on demand. When you request a new fact, a prompt is sent to Anthropic's servers containing only the list of recent fact topics you have seen (to avoid repetition). No personal information is included in these requests.

Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests. You can read it at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Advertising

The free version of Infinidge displays rewarded video ads (you choose to watch an ad to unlock an additional fact). These ads are served by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to serve ads. This is governed by Google's privacy policy. To limit ad tracking, you can adjust your device's advertising settings under Settings → Privacy → Ads on your Android device.

Premium users who have purchased or earned the Premium tier see no ads and are not subject to AdMob data collection within the app.

6. In-app purchases

If you choose to purchase Infinidge Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Google Play's terms and privacy policy govern all purchase transactions.

7. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Infinidge uses local notifications — these are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication. We do not send push notifications through external services.

8. Children's privacy

Infinidge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app and you have concerns, please contact us.

9. Data retention and deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. This permanently removes all locally stored data including your streak, bookmarks, and preferences. We have no copy of this data on our end.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since Infinidge does not collect personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by design. If you have a specific request or concern, please contact us and we will respond promptly.
